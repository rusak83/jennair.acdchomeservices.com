Factors that influence JennAir pricing

Columns, downdraft systems, and dual-fuel ovens sit at the top of the luxury category, so repairs depend on a few site-specific details.

Column sealed-system work

Twin compressors live behind custom cabinetry. When we have to evacuate refrigerant, pull a vacuum, and recharge with R600a or R134a, the labor pushes toward the upper end of the range and sometimes requires a return visit to monitor temperatures.

Range fuel type & modules

RISE and NOIR ranges combine gas cooktops with electric ovens, chrome griddles, and sometimes built-in downdraft lifts. Replacing ignition harnesses or control boards on those platforms takes longer than swapping a basic burner or bake element.

Flush installations

Many JennAir suites sit flush with stone or inset in furniture panels. If we need to remove trim, shrouds, or island panels to reach a control board, we budget extra time to keep the millwork pristine.

What to choose: DIY vs. hire an appliance repair tech?

Be careful working with electrical, plumbing or fuel connections. Putting yourself or your family in danger isn’t worth the money savings, especially when you’re dealing with chrome grates, dual-stack burners, or column sealed systems. If you need to replace a compressor, hinge kit, or induction module, call AC/DC – we’ll keep the luxury finish intact while restoring performance.

Column refrigeration rescue

JennAir column pairs rely on independent compressors, evaporators, and smart sensors to hold 34–38 °F in the fridge tower and sub-zero temps in the freezer. We stock inverter boards, evaporator fan motors, and door gaskets so columns in Southlake, Westlake, and Trophy Club get same-day attention.

Typical service calls

  • High-temp alarms or drifting temperatures after a power surge
  • Evaporator frost build-up from failed thermistors or clogged drains
  • Column door sagging so panels rub the cabinetry
  • Ice and water modules that stopped dispensing or began leaking
  • Compressor inverter fault codes and control board reboots

RISE & NOIR range repair

Dual-stacked burners, grills, griddles, and chrome-infused grates demand techs who understand JennAir calibration. We rebuild gas manifolds, replace ignition harnesses, and re-season griddles so dinner service stays on schedule.

Issues we solve fast

  • Simmer flames that flicker out because of clogged ports or low manifold pressure
  • 120V errors on dual-fuel ovens after a control relay shorts
  • Motorized grill and downdraft modules that no longer rise smoothly
  • Touchscreen or Wi-Fi modules freezing mid-cycle
  • Oven temperatures overshooting or lagging 30 °F from setpoint

What affects range repair cost?

Expect most JennAir range jobs to land between $425–$950. Chrome grates, downdraft lifts, and dual-fuel control boards raise the parts spend, while straightforward burner cleaning often stays on the low end.

JennAir wall & speed ovens

From 30" double wall ovens with V2 convection to stacked speed oven combos, we recalibrate probes, replace blower assemblies, and refresh seals so baking, roasting, and proofing stay precise.

Common problems

  • Broil or convection elements that no longer glow even though breakers are good
  • Stuck doors after a failed self-clean cycle
  • Cooling fan noise or vibration telegraphing through custom cabinets
  • Steam-crisp cycles that stop early because the reservoir sensor failed
  • Error codes F8E0, F2E1, or F6E4 triggered by user interface boards

Why homeowners call us

We carry OEM hinges, gaskets, relay boards, and temp sensors in the van, so most JennAir wall oven repairs wrap in a single visit across Flower Mound, Coppell, and Highland Village.

Downdraft ventilation & cooktops

JennAir practically invented telescoping downdraft for island kitchens. We keep blowers, grease filters, LED touch strips, and lift motors in stock so your open concept stays smoke-free.

Typical repairs

  • Downdraft canopy stuck halfway up because of worn gears
  • Ventilators running but pulling weak CFM because the plenum is clogged
  • Glass cooktops with cracked zones or unresponsive touch sliders
  • Induction modules throwing 7E/8E error codes

Keep the suite coordinated
